Le Fabuleux Destin d'Amelie Poulain

Synopsis

Following the life of a quirky and over-protected 20 something French girl, Amelie is the journey of a young woman searching for her purpose. Amelie Poulain, the daughter of a widowed doctor who believed her to have a heart defect and kept her from socializing with other children, is finally living on her own in Paris. She is emboldened by an act of kindness and embarks on a journey to discover the owner of a strange scrapbook, finding herself and love instead.

Released in 2001, Amelie has recieved international acclaim winning many awards across Europe. The soundtrack has also received international praise winning the World Soundtrack Award for Best Original Score of the Year in 2001.
